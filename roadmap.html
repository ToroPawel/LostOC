Plans | RoadMap | Aims

Alpha Version of "Ouster's metaverse": Development a metaverse platform called "Ouster's Vault" where NFT holders can visualize their NFTs as 3D characters. Implemention interactive features and games within the metaverse to provide entertainment for users.

Wallet Development: Creation a dedicated wallet for users to securely store their NFTs and other digital assets. Ensure the wallet supports multiple blockchains and provides a user-friendly interface for easy management of assets.

P2P Swap Functionality: Integration peer-to-peer (P2P) swap functionality within the wallet, allowing users to trade their NFTs and tokens directly with other users. Implemention secure and efficient smart contract-based swapping mechanisms.

Token Release: Launching our own token as part of our ecosystem. Definition the tokenomics, including token supply, distribution, and utility within "Ouster's metaverse".

Additional Utilities: Development and integration various utilities to enhance the user experience within "Ouster's metaverse". Examples of utilities could include virtual item customization, social features, virtual events, and more.

Marketplace Integration: Seting up a marketplace within "Ouster's metaverse" where users can buy, sell, and trade NFTs and other digital assets. Ensure a secure and user-friendly environment for conducting transactions.

Metaverse Expansion: Continuously expanding and improving "Ouster's metaverse" by adding new features, games, and interactive experiences. Collaboration with other GameFi projects to create partnerships and cross-platform integrations, enhancing the overall metaverse ecosystem.

Land Ownership: Allocation virtual lands to NFT holders within "Ouster's metaverse". Allowing users to sell or lease their lands to others, fostering a dynamic and user-driven economy. Enable users to build their own games or experiences on their allocated lands, encouraging creativity and community engagement.
